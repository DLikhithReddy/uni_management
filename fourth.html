Educational Programmes Offered

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is a four-year undergraduate engineering program that provides students with the theoretical and practical knowledge required to excel in various engineering fields. Throughout the program, students will gain a strong foundation in mathematics, science, and engineering principles. They will also specialize in a particular engineering discipline, such as civil, mechanical, computer science, or electrical engineering.

Master of Technology (M.Tech)

The Master of Technology (M.Tech) is a two-year postgraduate engineering program designed to equip students with advanced knowledge and expertise in a specific engineering field. The M.Tech program builds upon the foundation of knowledge gained in a Bachelor's in Engineering and allows students to delve into more specialized areas of engineering. Students will conduct research, complete coursework, and develop a deeper understanding of complex engineering concepts.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a four-year undergraduate program that equips students with the knowledge and skills needed for success in the business world. The BBA curriculum covers a wide range of business subjects, including accounting, finance, marketing, management, and economics. Students will gain practical experience through internships, case studies, and business simulations.
